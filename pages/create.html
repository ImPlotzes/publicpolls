Create a poll

Here you can create your own poll. Every poll has a few limits. These limits are listed below.

  • The question can be up to 128 characters long.
  • You have to provide at least 2 options, but no more than 15 options.
  • Your options can't be longer than 64 characters.
  • You have to add your name, this can be any name, as long as it's no longer than 32 characters.
